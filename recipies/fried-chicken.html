Home Fried Chicken Recipie

Description

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermlik
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Recipie Steps

  1. Make the Breading.Add flour, salt, and pepper into a bag. Shake the bag until well combined

  2. Dredge the ChickenPour buttermilk into a shallow bowl, then dip the chicken (on both sides) until it's completely soaked in buttermilk. Place the soaked chicken in the bag with flour mixture, seal, and shake well to coat. Transfer the coated chicken to a baking sheet, and cover. Allow the flour coating to become a paste-like consistency.

  3. Fry the ChickenPour the oil in a skillet and bring it to high heat. Brown the chicken on both sides, then reduce the heat, cover the skillet, and cook for about 30 minutes. Remove the cover, return the heat to high, and fry until extra crispy. Drain the chicken on paper towels.

Enjoy!
